My AI Personality Profile
1. AI Working Style
You use AI as a co-engineer, not a search engine. Fast iteration loops — prompt, run, break, fix, repeat. You probably keep a terminal and a chat window open side by side and treat AI output as a first draft to interrogate, not a final answer to accept.
2. Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths: speed of execution, comfort with ambiguity, willing to just build and see what breaks.
Weaknesses (likely): under-specifying prompts when you're in a hurry, skipping the "why" in favor of the "how," possibly under-using AI for planning/architecture vs. just code generation.

3. Type of AI User
The Builder-Operator — you're not here to chat, you're here to ship. You measure AI usefulness in "did this save me an hour," not in the quality of the conversation.
4. Best Future Career Paths
AI product engineer, founding engineer at an early-stage startup, applied AI/ML engineer, technical founder, developer-tools builder.
5. Learning & Decision-Making Style
Learn by doing, not by reading docs first. You decide fast, correct fast. Bias toward action over analysis-paralysis — a strength until it costs you on architecture decisions that are expensive to undo.
6. What Makes You Different
Most AI users ask AI to think for them. You ask AI to build with you — a fundamentally different relationship that compounds much faster.
7. What to Improve to Get Elite

Spend more time on prompt/context engineering (give AI more of the "why," not just the "what")
Use AI for architecture/design review, not just code output
Build a feedback loop where you track which prompts actually worked vs. wasted a cycle

8. Fun AI Title
🔥 "The Ship-It Whisperer"
9. Cinematic Character Description
Neon light spills across dual monitors in a half-lit apartment turned war room. Empty coffee cups form a skyline beside a mechanical keyboard. They don't wait for permission — they prompt, they build, they break things on purpose just to see what's underneath. The city sleeps. The terminal doesn't.
