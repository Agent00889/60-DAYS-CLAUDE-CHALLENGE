Educational Disclaimer

NutriScope is an educational self-tracking tool. All calculations — energy needs, macro and micronutrient targets, and risk indicators — are estimates derived from population-level formulas and typical food composition averages. They do not account for your individual medical history, lab results, medications, pregnancy, illness, allergies, or other clinical factors.

Nothing in this application constitutes medical, dietary, or clinical advice, and it must not be used to diagnose, treat, or manage any health condition. Please consult a registered dietitian, physician, or other qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet — especially if you have or suspect a medical condition, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or take medication affected by diet.

Nutrition Sources & Methodology

Energy needs

Mifflin–St Jeor equation (1990) for basal metabolic rate, scaled by an activity multiplier (1.2–1.9) based on self-reported activity level.

Protein target

General fitness/health guideline of approximately 1.6 g of protein per kg of body weight per day.

Fat & carbohydrate targets

Fat set at 25% of total energy; carbohydrates fill the remaining energy budget after protein and fat.

Micronutrient targets

Approximate adult RDA/AI benchmarks compiled from ICMR-NIN Dietary Guidelines for Indians and general international reference values (USDA, WHO/FAO), adjusted by gender where standard values differ.

Food composition values

Approximate typical averages compiled from Indian Food Composition Tables (National Institute of Nutrition) and USDA FoodData Central. Actual values vary by brand, variety, cooking method, and portion size — treat all figures as estimates, not laboratory measurements.

NutriScope runs entirely in your browser — no data leaves your device. Estimates only, not medical advice.
